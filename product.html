Product Information

Who is this Product for?

We are building Grocery Tracker to be for busy people who buy groceries and can't get a smart fridge. Our key customers we are targeting are young parents, college students, young workers living alone, and apartment dwellers or renters that want something like a smart fridge but can't afford to spend thousands of dollars on one.

What does it do?

Our Grocery Tracker product uses a camera that keeps track of what is in the fridge, tells you what you have and how much while youre out shopping, a database that tracks when a product in the fridge expires or close to expiring, and can automatically order common products when they expire or run out.

How it works

Grocery Tracker consists of a camera, a contact sensor, and a raspberry pi (or pi zero if we can eventually get one...). The camera will take images of what the user is putting in/taking out of the fridge and update a database which stores each users inventory accordingly. The contact sensor will determine when the camera is active and images taken by the video will be sent to a Computer Vision algorithm or API to determine what each grocery item is.

Key Benefits

  • The camera pod in the fridge scans products as they are added / removed
  • Lets you keep track of the amounts and expirations of each product and warns you when they run out / expire
  • Can be set to automatically restock certain groceries
  • Can scan a receipt instead of / in addition to using the camera to add new items to the database
  • Can scrape a recipe of the internet and tell you which ingredients you are missing
  • If you have multiple pods in the home, Grocery Tracker can tell you which fridge contains which food product
