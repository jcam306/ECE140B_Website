Product Features

Camera that tracks what you put into your fridge/cupboard

Benefit- The users' inventory of groceries will always be up to date.

Persona- The key persona(s) for this feature includes young parents, college students, young workers living alone, and apartment dwellers or renters that want a smart fridge but can't afford one. This benefit is important to them because it will allow them to easily know what is in their fridge at all times.

App that allows the user to see their current inventory of groceries and query using various filters

Benefit- The user will be able to know what is in their fridge or cupboard at all times and easily search for specific products/types of products.

Persona- The key persona(s) for this feature are mostly young parents and college students. This benefit is important to them because it will allow them to check what they currently have in at home while they are out shopping

Recipe Checker

Benefit- The user will be able to give the app a link to a recipe and the app will tell the user what they need for the recipe if they are missing any ingredients.

Persona- The key persona(s) for this feature includes young parents who cook meals for their family and indiviuals who are passionate about cooking at home. This benefit is important to them because it allows them to easily check if they have everything they need to make a specified recipe.

Expiration date tracking

Benefit- The app will estimate expiration date of items put into the fridge/cupboard and warn the user when items are expected to expire.

Persona- The key persona(s) for this feature include young parents, college students, and young workers living alone. This benefit is important to them because it will allow them to know when items in their inventory are about to expire so that they don't eat expired food or feed expired food to their family.

Automatic Restock

Benefit- The user will be able to enable prompts for automatic restocking of certain items.

Persona- The key persona(s) for this feature include college students and young workers living alone. This benefit is important to them because it will allow them to order groceries from the comfort of them home if they don't like to or don't have time to go grocery shopping.
