Grocery Tracker

We aim to make at home cooking and shopping for groceries more convienent for folks who are busy or don't want to spend time searching through their fridge or cupboards to check if they have particular food items. Our system will allow a user to scan their groceries or receipt using a camera which will then pass an image to a ML model which will identify the product and add it to a users current inventory. Our product will also track the expiration dates of the items scanned and alert the user to when products are close to expiring and give them an option to order more or add it to their shopping list. Grocery Tracker (name subject to change) will also be able to take user assigned recipes and query the database which stores their current items to determine whether they have everything they need.

Product Costs and Revenue Model

Costs

The price of manufacturing for our product is $39. On top of the cost of the materials and labor we must also consider the cost of ClarifAI ($30/month) which we use to distinguish items and the cost of the servers ($30-50/month at small scale) needed to run ClarifAI and store each customers' current inventory in MySQL database.

  • Raw Material: $36
    • Raspberry pi: $15
    • Camera: $17
    • Contact Sensor: $4
  • Labor: $3
    • 10 min to create one circuit.
    • 6 units/hour.
    • Employee cost: $18-20/hr.
    • Cost per item: ~$3.
  • Manufacturing: $39
    • Cost of materials: $36
    • Cost of labor: $3

Revenue Model

We expect to sell 5000-7000 units a year at $65 per item. This would result in a net revenue of $325k-$455k per year from online sales. We also can create revenue from advertisements on the phone app which users will use to view their current inventory of product. With an estimate of a customer using the app 4-5 times a month and 10000 users this would result in an average of ~$6/month of advertisement revenue per customer which equates to roughly $102k per year (source: https://www.google.com/adsense/start/#calculator). This brings our total revenue to $427k-557k per year if we estimate 10000 current users.

  • Online Sales: $325k-455k/year
    • Price of Hardware: $65
    • Sales per year: 5000-7000
  • Advertisements ~$102k/year
    • Estimated number of users: 10000
    • Estimated views/month per customer: 4-5
