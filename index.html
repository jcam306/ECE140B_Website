Grocery Tracker

We aim to make at home cooking and shopping for groceries more convienent for folks who are busy or don't want to spend time searching through their fridge or cupboards to check if they have particular food items. Our system will allow a user to scan their groceries or receipt using a camera which will then pass an image to a ML model which will identify the product and add it to a users current inventory. Our product will also track the expiration dates of the items scanned and alert the user to when products are close to expiring and give them an option to order more or add it to their shopping list. Grocery Tracker (name subject to change) will also be able to take user assigned recipes and query the database which stores their current items to determine whether they have everything they need.
